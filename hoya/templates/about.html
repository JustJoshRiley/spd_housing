Our Story

When considering what value we could bring to the world, we wanted something that could be used for the current person. Someone living in covid, looking for a new house, a new opportunity to move out, and live the remove life. Thats why we made Hoya.
Hoya is an app for developers by developers designed to give you the best market value in current home finding technology. 
Our talented team of developers at Hoya have made a home buying experience better than anything you’ve seen before. With a few clicks you’ll be on your way to finding out just how much you’ll be getting for that studio you’re so tired of - with the help of our (patent pending) price prediction algorithm. From there you can see other listings of homes in your area - or far, far away if you need a change. Try Hoya — we’ll get you home.
