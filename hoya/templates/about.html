Our Story

We considered what value we could bring to the world. We created something that would benefit people. Covid-19 presented a new way to look for a house to buy, sell, or browse. That is why we made Hoya.
Hoya is an app for people made by people who are determined to give you the best market value with the help of our price prediction model.
Our talented team of developers at Hoya have made the home buying experience easier to navigate with the goal of you finding a home. In a few clicks you may find out just how much that dust collecting studio is worth.
Not looking to sell? You can view listings of homes in your area, or anywhere around the globe.
